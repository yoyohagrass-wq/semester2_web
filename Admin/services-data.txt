1~Ma’edet El Rahman~Organizing free iftar tables during Ramadan to serve daily meals for families in need.
2~Orphan’s Day Celebration~We organize special events for orphans filled with joy, games, and gifts to bring happiness and a sense of belonging.
3~Ramadan Food Boxes~During Ramadan, we distribute essential food boxes to families, helping them prepare their daily meals with ease.
4~Mother’s Day Celebration~We honor mothers with gifts and gatherings to appreciate their efforts and spread love and gratitude in the community.
5~Paying Debts for the Needy~Our initiative helps families and individuals who are unable to repay their debts, relieving them from financial burdens.
6~Meat Distribution~Each Eid Al-Adha, we distribute fresh sacrificial meat to underprivileged families across various regions of Egypt.
7~Prosthetic Limbs Support~We provide artificial limbs and medical support to amputees, helping them regain independence and confidence.
8~Water Connection Projects~We work to supply clean and safe water to families in rural and underserved areas, improving their health and living conditions.
9~Distribution of Blankets~We distribute blankets and winter supplies to protect families from the cold during harsh winter months.
10~Bride Preparation Support~We assist brides from low-income families by providing essential home furnishings and wedding needs.
11~Home Renovation in Upper Egypt~We repair and rebuild homes for families living in poor conditions across Upper Egypt villages.
12~Water Purification Stations~We establish purification stations to provide clean, filtered water to rural communities lacking access to safe drinking water.
13~Hearing Aid Installation for Children~We provide hearing aids and medical support to children with hearing difficulties, helping them reconnect in their communities.
14~Cancer Patient Care~We offer support to cancer patients through treatment assistance and regular medical checkups to ease their journey toward recovery.
15~Dialysis Unit Support~We fund and equip dialysis units to ensure that patients with kidney failure receive the necessary treatment in a safe environment.
