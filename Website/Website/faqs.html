Frequently Asked Questions

How can I make a donation?

You can donate through our donation page by choosing a preset amount or entering a custom amount. All donations go directly toward supporting families in need.

Can I volunteer with the organization?

Yes! Visit our volunteering page and fill out the form. Once submitted, our team will contact you with available opportunities that match your interests.

Where does the foundation operate?

Al Mesbah Al Modie Foundation operates across several governorates in Egypt, including Cairo, Giza, and Upper Egypt regions.

How can I stay updated on your projects?

Follow us on Facebook or Instagram for the latest updates and events.

Can I donate items instead of money?

Absolutely. We accept in-kind donations such as clothes, food, and furniture. You can coordinate delivery with one of our branch offices listed on the branches page.
